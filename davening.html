Shacharit שחרית

Morning Blessings ברכות השחר

מוֹדֶה אֲנִי לְפָנֶיךָ מֶלֶךְ חַי וְקַיָּם, שֶׁהֶחֱזַרְתָּ בִּי נִשְׁמָתִי בְּחֶמְלָה. רַבָּה אֱמוּנָתֶךָ

Modeh Ani - I give thanks before You, living and eternal King, for You have returned my soul within me with compassion; abundant is Your faithfulness.

Offerings קרבנות

Readings about the Temple offerings

Verses of Praise פסוקי דזמרה

בָּרוּךְ שֶׁאָמַר וְהָיָה הָעוֹלָם, בָּרוּךְ הוּא...

Baruch She'amar - Blessed is He Who spoke and the world came into being...

Shema and Its Blessings קריאת שמע וברכותיה

שְׁמַע יִשְׂרָאֵל יְהוָה אֱלֹהֵינוּ יְהוָה אֶחָד

Shema Yisrael - Hear, O Israel: the Lord is our God, the Lord is One.

בָּרוּךְ שֵׁם כְּבוֹד מַלְכוּתוֹ לְעוֹלָם וָעֶד

Blessed be the name of His glorious kingdom forever and ever.

וְאָהַבְתָּ אֵת יְהוָה אֱלֹהֶיךָ בְּכָל־לְבָבְךָ וּבְכָל־נַפְשְׁךָ וּבְכָל־מְאֹדֶךָ...

You shall love the Lord your God with all your heart, with all your soul, and with all your might...

Amidah (Silent Prayer) עמידה

Stand facing Jerusalem. The Amidah is recited silently.

אֲדֹנָי שְׂפָתַי תִּפְתָּח וּפִי יַגִּיד תְּהִלָּתֶךָ

Lord, open my lips, and my mouth shall declare Your praise.

Weekday Amidah consists of 19 blessings. Full text available in traditional siddurim.

Ashrei אשרי

אַשְׁרֵי יוֹשְׁבֵי בֵיתֶךָ, עוֹד יְהַלְלוּךָ סֶּלָה

Praiseworthy are those who dwell in Your house; they will yet praise You forever.

Aleinu עלינו

עָלֵינוּ לְשַׁבֵּחַ לַאֲדוֹן הַכֹּל, לָתֵת גְּדֻלָּה לְיוֹצֵר בְּרֵאשִׁית...

It is our duty to praise the Master of all, to ascribe greatness to the Molder of creation...
